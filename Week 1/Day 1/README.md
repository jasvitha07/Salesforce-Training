## 1. What is CRM?

CRM means **Customer Relationship Management**.

It is software used to store customer information and manage sales.

Example:

* Customer names
* Phone numbers
* Sales details

---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce?

Companies use Salesforce to:

* Save customer data
* Track sales
* Talk to customers
* Manage work easily

---

## 3. Important Terms

### Account

Account = Company

Example: Infosys

---

### Contact

Contact = Person in the company

Example: Ramesh from Infosys

---

### Opportunity

Opportunity = Sales deal

Example: Selling software to Infosys

---

## 4. Real-World Example

| Salesforce  | Real World |
| ----------- | ---------- |
| Account     | Company    |
| Contact     | Person     |
| Opportunity | Deal       |

Example:

* Infosys → Account
* Ramesh → Contact
* Software Sale → Opportunity
